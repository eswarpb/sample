Matching Cards Game

How to Play:
Start the Game:
Enter your name in the input box.
Choose your difficulty level from the dropdown (Easy, Medium, or Difficult).
Click the Start Game button to begin.

Game Setup:
Based on your selected difficulty, the game will display a grid of face-down animal cards:
Easy: 8 cards (4 pairs)
Medium: 12 cards (6 pairs)
Difficult: 16 cards (8 pairs)
The cards are shuffled randomly each time.

Gameplay:
Click on a card to flip it over and reveal the animal image.
Flip over another card to find its matching pair.
If the two cards match, they stay face-up and count as a match.
If they don't match, both cards will flip back over after a short delay, allowing you to try again.

Track Your Progress:
The game keeps track of your Attempts (how many tries you've made) and your Time (how long you've been playing).
You also earn a Score: 10 points for each match.

Winning the Game:
The game is won when all pairs of cards are matched.
Upon winning, a message will display, congratulating you and showing how long it took to finish.

Reset the Game:
You can click the Reset Game button to restart and play again, or change the difficulty level to try a harder or easier version.
